INTRODUCTION

你好，我是王予呈。

目前就讀媒體傳達設計系大一。對我而言，設計是一場在繁複世界中去蕪存菁的過程。我目前在嘗試數位插畫、動態影像及 3D 空間構築，透過視覺語彙去傳遞核心情感。目前特別關注與社會議題、生命經驗相關的視覺實驗，期許自己能透過跨媒介的嘗試，創作出富含思維深度的作品。

Hi, I'm Yu-Cheng, a freshman majoring in Communications Design. To me, design is a process of cutting through the noise of a chaotic world. I am currently exploring digital illustration, motion graphics, and 3D space creation to convey core human emotions. Focusing on visual experiments inspired by social issues and life narratives, I aim to craft multi-disciplinary works with conceptual depth.

ALS SOCIAL ISSUES POSTERS

創作源於陪伴罹病家人的切身經歷。當我看著家人的四肢與脊椎漸漸無力、身體失控後仰，那種終將失去的恐懼，促使我以液體消逝的意象作為視覺起點。作品紀錄了直面病痛時、陪伴者最深沉的無力感，並試圖將這份說不出的心疼，轉化為視覺上的一記擁抱。

Rooted in my experience of caring for an ill family member. Witnessing the fading strength in their limbs and spine inspired me to use the imagery of fluid loss to manifest the fear of ultimate departure. The piece captures a caregiver’s profound helplessness, translating unspoken pain into a visual embrace.

MOSQUITO

以家中三隻貓咪的日常生活為靈感，將細碎的觀察轉化為動態分鏡實驗。在流暢的幀率與輕快節奏中，幽默捕捉貓隻與蚊子的趣味對峙，探討日常動態在影像敘事中的可能性。

Driven by daily observation of my three cats. This animation translates routine movements into dynamic visual storytelling, capturing the playful and witty battle between feline and insect.

NUDE MODEL

過程分為速寫與長時間觀察：前者著重於瞬時精準捕捉肢體比例；後者則引導我剝離表面線條，深入凝視肌肉出力與放鬆時的動態張力。這堂課使我學會以全新視角解構實體世界的力量架構。

A year-long training in charcoal and line drawing. The process balances gesture sketching and long-duration observation: the former captures precise body proportions in moments, while the latter guides me beyond surface details to analyze the dynamic tension of muscles contracting and relaxing. Through this, I learned to deconstruct the structural forces of the physical world.

BLENDER 3D MODELING

這是開學至今投入最多心力、也最感興趣的領域。從零基礎開始摸索，即使無數次的作業過程極度繁重，場景從無到有建立起來的純粹樂趣，仍讓我不管多累都想繼續做下去。對我而言，3D 建模已不僅僅是交差，而是驅使我將想像轉化為實體的真正熱忱。

The area I have invested in the most and found most captivating since day one. Starting from absolute zero, despite the endless and grueling assignments, the pure joy of building a scene from nothing keeps me pushing forward no matter how exhausted I am. Modeling has become far more than a task—it is the true passion driving me to turn imagination into reality.

OTHER WORKS

這是大一以來從零摸索的作品紀錄。在繁重習作中，我學會剝離表面，去凝視結構的力量與動態；並在疲憊中直面陪伴者的無力，將真實心疼轉化為視覺。這些嘗試，是我將直覺與情感提煉為實體設計的熱忱起點。

A record of my work and insights since freshman year, built from absolute zero. Through grueling assignments, I learned to look beyond the surface to grasp structural tension, transforming personal, helpless empathy into visual forms. These trials mark the raw beginning of my passion for turning intuition into reality.
