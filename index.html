INTRODUCTION

你好，我是王予呈。

目前就讀媒體傳達設計系大一。對我而言，設計是一場在繁複世界中去蕪存菁的過程。我目前在嘗試數位插畫、動態影像及 3D 空間構築，透過視覺語彙去傳遞核心情感。目前特別關注與社會議題、生命經驗相關的視覺實驗，期許自己能透過跨媒介的嘗試，創作出富含思維深度的作品。

Hi, I'm Yu-Cheng, a freshman majoring in Communications Design. To me, design is a process of cutting through the noise of a chaotic world. I am currently exploring digital illustration, motion graphics, and 3D space creation to convey core human emotions. Focusing on visual experiments inspired by social issues and life narratives, I aim to craft multi-disciplinary works with conceptual depth.
